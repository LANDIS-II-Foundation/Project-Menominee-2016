This archive contains LANDIS csv climate files for 8 MACA climate projections
for 38 climate regions in the VIFF spatial domain. 
The MACA projections
represent the following scenarios:

CSIRO-Mk3-6-0_r1i1p1_rcp85: hot-wet

HadGEM2-CC365_r1i1p1_rcp85: high tair and high prcp variance

HadGEM2-ES365_r1i1p1_rcp85: hot-dry
